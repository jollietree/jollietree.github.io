The importance of tree care.

Tree care, tree trimming and pruning your beloved tree is necessary to protect and maintain its health. Tree trimming and pruning a tree benefits the tree by removing dead, diseased and damaged branches that helps prevent further decay and insect infestation. These dead, damaged and diseased branches are dangerous and pruning them reduces the risk of damage to your property. Tree trimming also improves the structure and appearance of the tree and prevents it from developing weak and twisted branches.

How to identify dead, diseased and damaged branches.

  1. It’s not fall and the leaves are already falling.
  2. There are no leaves at all.
  3. The branches are falling off.

Tree removal: Knowing when it’s time.

What to look for?

  1. Mushrooms growing on the tree.
  2. Sogginess and/or rotting trunk.
  3. Big holes in the trunk, or hollow trunk.
  4. Bald spots with no bark on trunk and branches.

Emerald Ash Borer (EAB)

Emerald Ash Borer is identified by swirling curvy grooves bored into the trunk of the tree. This is a helpful article published at Wikipedia discussing the Environmental and economic impacts.
